About Our Website

ASIK or an abbreviation for Anak Ibu Sehat Indonesia Kuat is a website-based health program created to support government programs in reducing the infant mortality rate (IMR) and maternal mortality rate (MMR) in order to be able to achieve the 3rd SDGs target in 2030. On the website that we have provided For this reason, there are several educational features and health services according to family needs to ensure maternal health and child growth and development
